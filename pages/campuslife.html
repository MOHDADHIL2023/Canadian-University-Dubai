UNIVERSITY LIFE

STUDENTS AT CUD LIVE IN AN EXCITING COMUMUNITY

Offers Students a fascinating learning experience Canadian University Dubai (CUD) combines outstanding academics with an exciting campus culture. Over 120 Nationalities makes the creative campus home, and establishing the foundation for a colorful, various, and ulticultural educational experience.


Through Recreational & Social Events designed to develop innovative thinking, communication, & professional abilities, CUD enables the students to discover their passions. The aim is to provide students with the resourses required for a complete university experience, with opportunities & growth courses designed to help them achieve successful careers.


The focus on students' university is a buzzing of action, offering many clubs, plays, cultural events, and important outside efforts all scheduled to support strong systems for business and academic connections. These activities inspire academics to develop new talents and hone their current ones, changing each student's portfolio and prospects.

WHY ARE YOU STUDY IN DUBAI?

CUD is found at City Walk, in the center of Dubai. One of the towns in the world with the greatest rate of development is Dubai. Its multicultural tourist and student populations are growing, and the economy is growing faster. Many consider the city to be an appealing option due to its cosmopolitanism. Dubai is quickly becoming the world's leading digital metropolis driven by technology. Moreover, is among the Middle East's safest cities. Many foreigners and professionals are attracted to Dubai by its abundance of activities, including entertainment, beautiful beaches, a huge selection of excellent dining options, and a variety of thrilling experiences like parachuting, FOUR BY FOUR desert buggy, camel riding, water parks, skyscrapers, and more.
